Understanding ACEs (Adverse Childhood Experiences)

Childhood trauma affects mental, physical, and social health in adulthood. It's crucial to understand ACEs and their impact to better support those affected.

The ACE Study

"The Adverse Childhood Experiences (ACE) Study reveals the profound impact of childhood trauma on lifelong health. This research shows that childhood abuse and household dysfunction are both widespread and strongly linked to many leading causes of death, including heart disease, cancer, stroke, depression, and addiction. By highlighting how early trauma can contribute to serious health issues, the ACE Study emphasizes the need to address and prevent childhood abuse for a healthier society." - Dr. Vincent Felitti
